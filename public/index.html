Cars & User Routes

Show All Cars In The Website

  • Get 10 products by page number: /cars/?page=2 (GET)
  • Get X product per one page: (default: 9) /cars/?perPage=3 (GET)
  • Search porduct by name or information /cars/search/?s=bmw (GET)
  • Get proudct by Category-name: /cars/:category (GET)
  • Price range /cars/prices/?max=x&min=y&page=z (GET)
  • Sort by ...(default "_id") /cars/?sort=company (GET)
  • Sort form smaller to biger (default: biger-smaller) /cars/?reverse=yes (GET)
